GB3GU Maritime Forecast

Channel Islands inshore waters forecast announced twice daily on GB3GU (ASL3 node 43172, Guernsey) using the Met Office inshore waters forecast, Guernsey land temperatures from gov.gg, and Piper TTS (Jenny, en_GB voice). The underlying script supports all 19 UK inshore waters areas and optional GPS-based region selection.

Morning — 07:30 daily

"Good morning, here is the Channel Islands 24 hour maritime forecast…"

Evening — 19:30 daily

"Good evening, here is the Channel Islands outlook for the following 24 hours…"
